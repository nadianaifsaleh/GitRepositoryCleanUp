♥ Salted date molasses & fennel cookies ♥

 
  •  Description
  •  Ingredients
  •  Directions

Description

Salted date molasses & fennel cookies was inspired by sugar cane molasses cookies. 
(known as molasses cookies) Date molasses is very well known for being aromatic & flavorful.

Emirati spirit, and fennel cuz we usually add fennel to date desserts in UAE.
and of course afternoon Arabic coffee (gahwa).

Ingredients

  • (219g) 1 ¾cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground fennel
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ¾teaspoon sea salt
  • (84g) 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • (200g) 1 cup granulated sugar, divided
  • (55g) ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 large eğğ
  • 60ml) ¼ date cup molasses
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom

Directions

Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, ginger, fennel, cloves, salt in 
a bowl..set a side Cream butter, 5 tablespoons granulated sugar, and brown 
sugar in a large bowl; beat with a mixer at medium speed 5 minutes or until 
fluffy!. Add eğğ beat 30 seconds. Add date molasses; beat 
just until combined. Add flour mixture to butter mixture; beat at low speed 
or just until combined. Cover and chill 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350°. Shape dough into 22-24 ( walnut size) I used 
1 oz ice cream scoop. Roll balls in remaining 3 tablespoons sugar mixed
with ½ teaspoon of cardamom ; place apart on baking sheets covered 
with parchment paper. Bake at 350° for 12 minutes or just until set. 
Cool 3 minutes on pan; remove to a wire rack.
